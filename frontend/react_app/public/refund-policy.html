Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: October 14, 2024

Last Updated: October 14, 2024

Quick Summary: You can cancel anytime. Refunds are available within 7 days for unused services. No refunds for used services or after 7 days.

1. Subscription Cancellation

1.1 How to Cancel

  • Self-Service: Cancel anytime through your account dashboard
  • Email: Send cancellation request to support@specter-legal.com
  • Effective Date: Cancellation takes effect at the end of current billing period
  • Access: You retain access until subscription expires

1.2 What Happens After Cancellation

  • No further charges will be made
  • Account reverts to Free tier after expiry
  • Data is retained as per Privacy Policy
  • You can reactivate subscription anytime

2. Refund Policy

Scenario Refund Eligibility Processing Time
Within 7 days, unused service 100% refund 5-7 business days
Within 7 days, partially used Pro-rated refund 5-7 business days
After 7 days No refund N/A
Technical issues (our fault) Full refund or credit 3-5 business days
Billing errors Full correction 3-5 business days

3. Refund Conditions

3.1 Eligible for Refund

  • Unused Services: If you haven't used any premium features
  • Technical Issues: Service unavailable due to our technical problems
  • Billing Errors: Incorrect charges or duplicate payments
  • Unauthorized Charges: Charges made without your consent

3.2 NOT Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind after using services
  • Requests made after 7 days of purchase
  • Violation of Terms of Service
  • Free tier usage (no payment made)
  • Partial months (except for technical issues)

4. Pro-Rated Refunds

Calculation Method

Refund Amount = (Subscription Fee ÷ 30 days) × Unused Days

Example: If you cancel a ₹299 Lite plan after 10 days of use:
Refund = (₹299 ÷ 30) × 20 unused days = ₹199.33

5. Refund Process

5.1 How to Request

  1. Email: Send request to refunds@specter-legal.com
  2. Include:
    • Account email address
    • Transaction ID or payment reference
    • Reason for refund request
    • Date of purchase
  3. Response: We'll respond within 24-48 hours

5.2 Processing Timeline

  • Review: 1-2 business days
  • Approval: Email notification sent
  • Processing: 3-7 business days via Razorpay
  • Bank Credit: 5-10 business days (depends on your bank)

6. Payment Method Refunds

Payment Method Refund Method Timeline
Credit/Debit Card Original card 5-7 business days

Net Banking Original bank account 3-5 business days
Wallet Original wallet 1-2 business days

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Technical Service Issues

  • Extended Downtime: Automatic service credit or refund
  • Data Loss: Full refund if we cannot restore your data
  • Feature Unavailability: Pro-rated credit for affected period

7.2 Account Termination

  • Voluntary: No refund for remaining period
  • Policy Violation: No refund, immediate termination
  • Our Decision: Pro-rated refund for unused period

8. Dispute Resolution

8.1 Internal Process

  1. First Contact: Email support@specter-legal.com
  2. Escalation: If unsatisfied, request manager review
  3. Final Review: Senior management decision within 7 days

8.2 External Options

  • Consumer Forum: File complaint with local consumer court
  • Banking Ombudsman: For payment-related disputes
  • Razorpay Dispute: Contact Razorpay for payment issues

9. Free Tier Policy

Free Tier Users: Since no payment is involved, cancellation simply means stopping usage. No refund process applies to free accounts.
